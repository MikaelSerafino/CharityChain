Frequently Asked Questions

What is KPGT and how do I get it?

KPGT is the native token on the Mandala Chain used for all donations on CharityChain. You can acquire KPGT through cryptocurrency exchanges or swap other tokens for KPGT using decentralized exchanges (DEX) on the Mandala network.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Tax deductibility depends on your local regulations and the campaign's status. For registered nonprofits, donations may be tax-deductible. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for specific guidance based on your jurisdiction.

How do I know campaigns are legitimate?

Every campaign is recorded on the blockchain with the creator's wallet address, making them fully traceable. While we don't verify individual stories, the blockchain provides complete transparency of fund flow. We encourage donors to do their own research and contact campaign creators directly if they have questions.

Can I get a refund on my donation?

Due to the nature of blockchain transactions, donations are final and cannot be reversed. This ensures the integrity of the system. We strongly recommend reviewing campaigns carefully before donating and only contributing amounts you're comfortable with.

What happens to the 2% platform fee?

The 2% fee covers platform maintenance, blockchain transaction costs, hosting, security audits, and ongoing development. This minimal fee ensures CharityChain remains sustainable while keeping most of your donation going directly to the cause.

Do I need technical knowledge to use CharityChain?

Not at all! If you can use a website, you can use CharityChain. Simply connect your wallet (we'll guide you), browse campaigns, and donate. Creating a campaign is just as simple with our step-by-step process. No blockchain or cryptocurrency expertise required.
